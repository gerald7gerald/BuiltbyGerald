Case Study

Inside KP12 Performance

A closer look at the front-end and back-end systems built for this platform.

Client-Facing Homepage

The homepage splits into three clear paths — Athletics, Training, and Nutrition — so visitors immediately land on the service that matches what they're looking for, without digging through menus.

Program & Pricing Pages

Each training program has its own page with live spot availability, session-pack pricing, and a direct booking link — built so pricing and capacity update dynamically instead of being hardcoded.

Employee Scheduling Dashboard

On the back end, employees log into their own dashboard to build out their weekly availability, edit or clear sessions, and manage bookings — all synced with what clients see on the public site.

Session Booking

Clients pick their own sessions directly from a live calendar, with real-time spot availability for each day and time slot — so booking updates instantly without any back-and-forth.

Client & Athlete Management

Employees can also manage a roster of athletes tied to their account, track completed sessions, and reach clients directly by email — all built into the same login-protected dashboard, without needing a separate email client.
